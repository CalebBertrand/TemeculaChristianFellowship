What We're About

Fellowship.

Temecula Christian Fellowship is more than a building or a Pastor or an organization. It is a group of people who have decided to follow Jesus together. We believe that following Jesus is more than gathering together once a week to check something religious off the list so that we can feel better about ourselves. We believe that fellowship is the key. Fellowship simply means friendship.
